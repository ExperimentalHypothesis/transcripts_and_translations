mantra is nothing but a thought which is vibrated. Any thought that is
vibrated is a mantra.

it works. If it does not work, then your mantra has no meaning.
(Diwali Puja, London, 6. November 1983)
There should be complete purity in the recitation of mantras to
awaken the deities and your heart must be involved in it.
