 ENGLISH TRANSLATION (Marathi Talk) I have not told these people what is the greatness of Rahuri. Rahuri was part of the kingdom of Shalivahanas. During bygone era demon known as Rahu lived at this place. He was worst evil and tortured the people. In order to annihilate this demon Devi took incarnation of Mahalaxmi and killed him at Rahuri. In the word Rahuri “Ri" is a form of Mahalaxmi and that form of Mahalaxmi killed him. The Devi Mahatyma being the description of Mahakali we do not find the mention of Mahalaxmi killing demon Rahu, in that book. Rahu was thrashed with a spade at Musalwadi, so he fled and then screamed at place, which later came to be known as Aradgaon. Then he fell to the ground and died at this place. Hence the greatness of Rahuri and we have to understand how big it is. Thousands of years later when King Babruvahan of Sahlivahan dynasty was ruling, he defeated king Vikramaditya. 99 Vikramaditya was a king of Ujjain. He introduced a calendar in his own name. Later the Shalivahan king Babruvahan also introduced his own calendar known as the Shalivahan Shaka. According to this calendar about 1988 number of years have passed (in 1984).( Shri Mataji spoke in English for a while and then changed to talking in Marathi.) I was telling them that after the introduction of this calendar the Shalivan king founded his kingdom and lived there from the day of the Gudhi Padva. (Gudhi is a symbol of auspiciousness) Since you know all this I did not tell you and explained to them in English. With respect to the Gudhi Padva the shawl has great significance. The Shalivahan kings always carried the Goddess's shawl with them. You know the significance of Devi's shawl. You know it gives Her beauty. With the shawl She protects Herself from cold and wind. Also with it She protects Her chastity, as well as if any dirt is about touch Her body it is prevented. So, a shawl which is offered to Goddess symbolizes Her protection, meaning since you protect us we offer you this. Further if the children are sleeping or are sick or have a problem, then She covers them with the shawl. So shawl is very important. You know, in the past it was a practice with all people in Maharashtra to take shawl over their bodies. Now that we have become modern we do not take it. (Shri Maataji spoke in English for a little while and then resumed Marathi speech). The calendar that the Shalivahan king introduced was accepted by Tilak and so we have. It contains subtle information. According to that calendar present times is the Kritayuga and not the Kaliyuga. The Kritayuga means the Param Chaitanya is active during this time and it commenced since the time of my birth. See how subtle it is, what subtle point it has caught.! Hence Tilak declared it to be true and the correct calendar to be followed. Hence It is followed all over Maharashtra. It is little different, but Sahaja Yogis should refer to it. The one introduced by Vikramaditya is superficial and not correct. Only the Shalivahan calendar should be used. I am going to tell in the North India as well, to follow the Shalivahan calendar since it is subtle. Now, a program will also be held at Nandgaon which is my parental hometown i.e. my parental hometown. Lakhuji Jadhav ( Father of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaja's mother, to whose dynasty Shri Mataji's mother belonged ) tired of harassment came down and settled at Nandagaon. He knew that Mughal king Aurangzeb was afraid of the Shalivahanas. Aurangzeb came to Ahmednagar not to Rahuri .None of Muslim kings came. The reason was the Mughal king was afraid of Devi's devotees who lived at Rahuri. So Lakhuji Jadhav came down to Nandgaon and established here the capital of his small kingdom. So be it. They have offered lands to me and so the history is repeating. Sahaja Yoga work will start over there. They have offered a number of plots. Thus we have to restart Sahaja Yoga from the subtle. Sahaja Yoga which was practiced in the past was at the gross level and now we want to restart it in the subtle. Hence Rahuri has great importance. At the moment let us do the Bandhan. mother's 4

I have not told these people what is the greatness of Rahuri. Rahuri was part of the kingdom of
Shalivahanas. During bygone era demon known as Rahu lived at this place. He was worst evil and
tortured the people. In order to annihilate this demon Devi took incarnation of Mahalaxmi and killed
him at Rahuri. In the word Rahuri “Ri" is a form of Mahalaxmi and that form of Mahalaxmi killed
him. The Devi Mahatyma being the description of Mahakali we do not find the mention of Mahalaxmi
killing demon Rahu, in that book. Rahu was thrashed with a spade at Musalwadi, so he fled and then
screamed at place, which later came to be known as Aradgaon. Then he fell to the ground and died at
this place. Hence the greatness of Rahuri and we have to understand how big it is. Thousands of years
later when King Babruvahan of Sahlivahan dynasty was ruling, he defeated king Vikramaditya.

Vikramaditya was a king of Ujjain. He introduced a calendar in his own name. Later the
Shalivahan king Babruvahan also introduced his own calendar known as the Shalivahan Shaka.
According to this calendar about 1988 number of years have passed (in 1984).( Shri Mataji spoke in
English for a while and then changed to talking in Marathi.) I was telling them that after the
introduction of this calendar the Shalivan king founded his kingdom and lived there from the day of
the Gudhi Padva. (Gudhi is a symbol of auspiciousness) Since you know all this I did not tell you and
explained to them in English.
With respect to the Gudhi Padva the shawl has great significance. The Shalivahan kings always
carried the Goddess's shawl with them. You know the significance of Devi's shawl. You know it gives
Her beauty. With the shawl She protects Herself from cold and wind. Also with it She protects Her
chastity, as well as if any dirt is about touch Her body it is prevented. So, a shawl which is offered to
Goddess symbolizes Her protection, meaning since you protect us we offer you this. Further if the
children are sleeping or are sick or have a problem, then She covers them with the shawl. So shawl is
very important. You know, in the past it was a practice with all people in Maharashtra to take shawl
over their bodies. Now that we have become modern we do not take it. (Shri Maataji spoke in English
for a little while and then resumed Marathi speech). The calendar that the Shalivahan king introduced
was accepted by Tilak and so we have. It contains subtle information. According to that calendar
present times is the Kritayuga and not the Kaliyuga. The Kritayuga means the Param Chaitanya is
active during this time and it commenced since the time of my birth. See how subtle it is, what subtle
point it has caught.! Hence Tilak declared it to be true and the correct calendar to be followed. Hence It
is followed all over Maharashtra. It is little different, but Sahaja Yogis should refer to it. The one
introduced by Vikramaditya is superficial and not correct. Only the Shalivahan calendar should be
used. I am going to tell in the North India as well, to follow the Shalivahan calendar since it is subtle.
