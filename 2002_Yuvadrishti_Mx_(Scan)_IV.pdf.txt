six months old. As she was returning in a tonga she put Shri Mataji on the back seat and called the
tonga-
driver to help her climb the step to get to the back seat, As the driver came to help her the horse for
unknown reason started to move the carriage before her mother could get into the tonga. So a situation
arose where the horse was carrying the tonga with Shri Mataji in the back seat, all alone. The road on
which the tonga was travelling terminated at a T-junction where if he had continued to go straight, he
would have fallen into a ditch. To the surprise of everyone the horse reduced its speed and took a left turn,
towards the bungalow and then again started galloping Then the horse stopped by himself right in front of
of the bungalow, looking backwards as if to see whether the occupant of the tonga was safe or
the
